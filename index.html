About Us

ATK Poultry and Farm is dedicated to providing farm-fresh eggs, healthy chickens, and high-quality organic feed. Our goal is to promote healthy living through sustainable poultry farming.

Our Products

Country Chicken

Farm-fresh, healthy, and organic free-range country chicken raised in a natural environment.

Broiler Chicken

Fresh, tender, and hygienically processed high-quality broiler chicken meat.

Fresh Eggs

Premium quality, farm-fresh eggs that are clean, organic, and rich in nutrients.

Chicks

Active, healthy, and fully vaccinated day-old chicks of premium breeds.

Poultry Feed

Highly nutritious and well-balanced feed formulated for healthy poultry growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our delivery, orders, visits, and payments.

Yes, home delivery is available! We deliver across Tamil Nadu, Kerala, and Karnataka. Depending on the size of your order, we dispatch via Bike, Small Pickup, Mini Lorry, or Cold Storage Van. Please check our Transportation section for mileage charges and availability.

There is no strict minimum order for farm gate pickups! However, for home deliveries, minimum order requirements apply based on your location and the selected delivery vehicle. For wholesale bulk orders, please contact us directly for customized logistics.

Yes! We welcome customers, distributors, and farming enthusiasts to visit our farm. To ensure safety and prevent disruption to our daily operations, all farm visits must be scheduled at least 24-48 hours in advance. Please reach out to us to book your visit.

We accept a variety of convenient payment methods, including Cash on Delivery (COD) / Cash on Pickup, all major UPI apps (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, Amazon Pay), and Direct Bank Transfers (IMPS/NEFT/RTGS) for bulk orders.
